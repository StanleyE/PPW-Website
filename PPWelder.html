Pink Princess Welding

Independent welder for hire to help with Small Home Repair, Motorcycle & Car body work and Art Projects

The Name


Well, safe to say I have been laughed at due to the name, thankfully I have a good sense of humor. Why Pink Princess Welding? My daughter helped me pick the name. Despite always being dressed head to toe in pink, and my wife's quite disapproval of the grease stains. My daughter is always in the garage helping me work. From fetching tools to 'critiquing' my latest work, she has been 'helping' since she could sit upright. When I finally decided to open my own business, I asked for help with the naming and that was that (despite cooler names being suggested by both me and my wife).
